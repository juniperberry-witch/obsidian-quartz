Along with [[Der Yuulsege (Yule)]], this is the highest holiday on the Urglaawe calendar. The night of April 30 into May 1 is observance of the return of [[Holle]] from the Furious Host (Wild Hunt) that began on [[Allelieweziel (Halloween)]] to the soil. Her return brings the land to back to full life.

Experience the joy of life, including the joy of planting new life in the ground. Make the planting a spiritual, loving event. Revel in friends, family, kindred, hobbies, and things you enjoy.
