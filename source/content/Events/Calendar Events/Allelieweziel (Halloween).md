Allelieweziel ushers in the new Germanic spiritual year, starting with the Dark Half. Now is the time of asking ourselves what we can do to improve ourselves (spiritually, physically, mentally, in all ways) on our life's journey and of identifying what we need to banish from our lives in the next year.

Holle's departure on the Furious Host (Wild Hunt) is reflected in the death of the fertility of the soil. Allelieweziel is the death phase of the life-death-rebirth cycle. As Holle sets off upon the Furious Host, She is followed by an entourage of the lands spirits and the souls of the recently departed. Throughout the dark half of the year, Holle hunts for stray souls. As She finds them, they join the Furious Host until She brings them to her figurative mill to "grind" them into their next life.
