Der Berchtaslaaf is, perhaps, akin to this time of introspection and the forming of New Year's Resolutions. We observed, recognized, and even embraced these aspects of ourselves during the time of Belsnickel and Krampus prior to Yule. There is also value in assessing this side of us now we work on improving ourselves in the coming year. The Berchtaslaaf, or the Progression of [[Berchta]] is a feast that includes gruel and fish, which is Berchta's prescribed meal. Urglaawer typically make the fare to consist of herring and oatmeal or dumpling and herring meal (Grimm, Teutonic 273).
