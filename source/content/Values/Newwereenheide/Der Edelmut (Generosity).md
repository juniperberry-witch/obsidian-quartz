Heathen principles contain the concept of "a gift for a gift," so a gift from a Heathen is met by a return gift, even if the values of the gifts are not equal. This principle is an aspect of Fruchsfriede (frith) and of open lines of communication, thus signaling peace between the two parties. What of the community outside of the Heathens, though? Fruchsfriede indicates that we have a responsibility to everyone. Foremost are our children, spouses, kinfolk, and then we move to more distant family, friends, and acquaintances. Simply by being a part of the human community, we owe even strangers some frith.
