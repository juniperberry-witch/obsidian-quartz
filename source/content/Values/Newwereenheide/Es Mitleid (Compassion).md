The focus of Mitleid is that of an appropriate compassion for others who are in a position through no fault of their own or who have seen their errors and are attempting to correct them. Compassion for irresponsibility is detrimental to the whole of the community. However, equally detrimental is providing no path to redemption for those who are trying to straighten themselves out. They become stuck in a quagmire of their mistakes, even if they are striving to distance themselves from their prior behavior.

Mitleid can result in our lending a hand, when feasible in order to help strengthen our community overall.
