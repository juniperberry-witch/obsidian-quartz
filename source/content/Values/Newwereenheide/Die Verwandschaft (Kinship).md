The virtue of Kinship reflects the need that all of us have to belong to a community. Despite the efforts of the consumer culture to separate and divide people in order to increase corporate bottom lines, it is unnatural for humans not to live and to exist in a social setting. Kinship, along with other virtues, can help to create an environment of security, trust, and interdependence among the members of a community. Even while we embrace Self‐Reliance (Selbschtzuverdraue), we also embrace that community interdependence, which is reflected in the Heathen concepts of gift exchanges and frith.
