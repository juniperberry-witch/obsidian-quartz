# What is Braucherei
Braucherei is the age-old heirloom healing practice that has been passed down through the centuries.

Braucherei has many unbroken roots in pre-Christian practices, but it also has layers from other cultures or traditions with which it came into contact. Braucherei, though, is not a religion; it is a health and healing practice.
