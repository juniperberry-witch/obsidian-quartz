 Butzemann is a scarecrow who has been spiritually activated by receiving the breath of life during what is called [[Der Kannsege (Ceremony of the Corn)]]. This ceremony takes place on [[Der Oschdresege (Groundhog Day)]], which is also the day upon which we celebrate the female creative powers and motherhood. 
 
 Between [[Erntfescht (Harvest Home)]] and [[Allelieweziel (Halloween)]], the Butzemann is released from his duties. Typically, around the time of Allelieweziel, his role changes. His need to guard the land is passed. He now takes on the embodiment of the sacrifices that males make for their families and for the community.
