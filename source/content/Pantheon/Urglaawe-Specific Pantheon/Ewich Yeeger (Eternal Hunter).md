Ewich Yeeger is generally seen as harsh yet helpful, particularly given the fact that He rescued early settlements. However, He is also viewed as a large entity, who is running errands that defy our comprehension, so it would be easy for Him to roll over someone without Him even realizing he had done it.

Often considered to be the same deity as [[Holler]].
# Deity Details
**Deity Of**:
- God constantly on the hunt

**Associations**:
- Blue Mountain, Oley Furnace, Yellow Mountain, South Mountain, and Neversink Mountain
