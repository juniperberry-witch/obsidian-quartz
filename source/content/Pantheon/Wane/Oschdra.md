# Deity Details
**Deity Of**: Herald of Spring, Goddess of Dawn

**Associations**: [[Egg]], [[Hare]]

> [!quote] Distelfink Sippschaft 
> Very little about Oschdra in the way of ancient lore has survived to reach us. Deitsch oral lore provides some additional insights. Oschdra is counted in Braucherei among the Wane. From Grimm (IV, 1371) we know her name lives on in places in the German lands. We know She is considered to be “White Lady” (III, 908) Along with Holle, Berchta, and others, and She heralds the arrival of Spring. Like our ancestors, we yearn for Oschdra and the return of Spring. She is the goddess of the Dawn, who brings color and joy back to a winter weary world. Our modern conveniences separate us from the cycle of the seasons Thus, we do not, or cannot, understand fully what the return of Spring meant to our forebears. Yet we do understand and share the desire for, and love of, Earth's beauty and bounty. It is the same longing for the end of winter.
